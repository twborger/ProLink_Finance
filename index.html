Do You Really Need a 20% Deposit?

30 March 2026
One of the biggest misconceptions among first home buyers is that a 20% deposit is required to purchase a property. While a larger deposit can provide greater flexibility, many eligible Australians can now enter the market with as little as a 5% deposit through the Australian Government's 5% Deposit Scheme.

The scheme allows eligible buyers to avoid Lenders Mortgage Insurance (LMI), potentially saving thousands of dollars and helping them purchase sooner. Understanding your borrowing capacity, available government support and lender options can make a significant difference when planning your first home purchase.
